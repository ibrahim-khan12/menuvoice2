The problem

A menu assumes you can read it. For millions of people that means asking a stranger to read dinner aloud, or ordering the same safe thing every time.
Independence
Choose your own meal, without handing the decision to whoever's next to you.
Privacy

Dignity
No assistance theater. Just the facts you need to choose for yourself.
